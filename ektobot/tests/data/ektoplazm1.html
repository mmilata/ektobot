Globular – Magnitudes Of Order

Free Music | Downtempo, Psy Dub | November 27, 2013 | Posted by Basilisk

01 - Synchronicity City 3.0 (77 BPM)

Magnitudes Of Order is the second full-length album from Globular (Morison Bennett), following up on A Self-Fulfilling Prophecy, his masterful debut. The album is a celebration of the vast scales of the universe around us, from the macro: up to the skies; the solar system; the galaxy; the universe and the possibility of a multiverse, to the micro: plant-life; cells; complex molecules and the seemingly infinite regression of mind-blowingly small stuff. By blending elements of electronica, dub, and world music, Globular’s sound has come into it’s own with this latest release, and is now easily recognizable and instantly distinguishable from the sea of electronic music currently available. Pull up a bed, light up, and get lost in sound…
